## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Mid-Response Steering**: GPT-5.4 introduces the ability to redirect a model while it's still generating a response — but what does that actually mean? Traditional LLM interactions are fire-and-forget: you send a prompt, you wait for the full response. Mid-response steering lets you interrupt and course-correct in real time, like telling a colleague "actually, focus on the security implications instead" while they're still presenting. This matters because it turns generation from a batch process into an interactive one, dramatically reducing wasted tokens and time when the model starts heading in the wrong direction.
