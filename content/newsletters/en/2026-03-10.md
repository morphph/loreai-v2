## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Reasoning Models vs. Base Models**: O3, O4 Mini, and DeepThink are "reasoning models" — they spend extra compute at inference time to think through problems step by step before answering. Unlike base models that predict the next token directly, reasoning models generate an internal chain-of-thought, trading speed and cost for accuracy on hard problems. The tradeoff is real: a reasoning model might take 30 seconds and cost 10x more than a base model for a question that doesn't need deep thinking. Use reasoning models for math, logic, and complex analysis; use base models for drafting, summarization, and anything where "good enough fast" beats "perfect slowly."
