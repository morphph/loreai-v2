## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Model Distillation (and Why Anthropic Is Furious)**: Distillation is a technique where a smaller "student" model learns to mimic a larger "teacher" model by training on the teacher's outputs rather than raw data. It's legitimate when done with your own models — but when someone queries your API millions of times to harvest responses and train a competitor, that's a different story. The key issue: model outputs contain compressed knowledge that took billions of dollars to develop, and there's currently no reliable way to watermark or protect them at scale. Anthropic's accusation against DeepSeek and others highlights a fundamental tension — open APIs enable innovation but also enable theft.
