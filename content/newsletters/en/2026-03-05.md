## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Model Distillation**: When a smaller "student" model is trained to mimic a larger "teacher" model's outputs, that's distillation. Instead of learning from raw data, the student learns from the teacher's responses — capturing much of the capability at a fraction of the compute cost. This is why Anthropic's accusation matters: if competitors systematically query Claude to build training data, they're essentially stealing years of research and billions in training costs. Distillation is a legitimate technique when done with your own models, but doing it to a competitor's API at industrial scale crosses into IP theft territory.
