## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Model Distillation**: Distillation is the process of training a smaller "student" model to replicate the behavior of a larger "teacher" model by learning from its outputs rather than from raw training data. The student doesn't need to match the teacher's architecture or size — it just learns to produce similar responses, often achieving 80-90% of the teacher's quality at a fraction of the compute cost. Today's Anthropic disclosure shows the dark side: competitors can use your API as an unwitting teacher, effectively stealing your model's capabilities through millions of carefully crafted queries. The defense involves output fingerprinting (embedding detectable patterns in responses) and behavioral analysis to catch systematic extraction patterns.
