## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Mixture of Experts (MoE)**: Traditional models activate every parameter for every token — a 70B model uses all 70B parameters per forward pass. MoE models like Qwen3.5-35B-A3B split parameters into "expert" groups and only activate a subset per token (3B active out of 35B total). A gating network decides which experts are relevant for each input. The result: you get the knowledge capacity of a much larger model at the inference cost of a small one. The tradeoff is higher memory to load the full model, even though compute per token stays low — so MoE shines when you have the VRAM but need fast inference.
