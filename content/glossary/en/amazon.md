# Amazon — AI Glossary

**Amazon** is a global technology company whose cloud division, Amazon Web Services (AWS), operates one of the largest AI infrastructure platforms in the world. Through services like **Amazon Bedrock**, **SageMaker**, and **Trainium** custom chips, Amazon provides the compute, tooling, and model-hosting layer that powers a significant share of enterprise AI workloads. Amazon is also a major investor in [Anthropic](/glossary/anthropic), the company behind [Claude](/glossary/claude).

## Why Amazon Matters

AWS controls roughly a third of the global cloud market, making Amazon a gatekeeper for how most companies deploy and scale AI. **Amazon Bedrock** gives enterprises API access to foundation models from Anthropic, Meta, Mistral, and others — without managing infrastructure. This model-marketplace approach lets teams swap between providers with minimal code changes.

Amazon's multi-billion-dollar investment in Anthropic has deepened the integration between Claude models and AWS services, making Bedrock one of the primary ways organizations access Claude at scale. For context on Claude's latest capabilities, see our coverage of [Anthropic's memory upgrades](/blog/anthropic-claude-memory-upgrades-importing).

## How Amazon Works in AI

Amazon's AI strategy spans three layers:

- **Infrastructure**: Custom silicon — **Trainium** for training and **Inferentia** for inference — competes with [NVIDIA](/glossary/nvidia) GPUs on cost-per-token for large-scale workloads
- **Platform**: **SageMaker** handles end-to-end ML workflows (data labeling, training, deployment), while **Bedrock** provides managed access to third-party foundation models
- **Applications**: AI features embedded across Amazon's consumer products — Alexa, product recommendations, logistics optimization, and automated warehousing

## Related Terms

- **[Anthropic](/glossary/anthropic)**: AI safety company and Claude's creator, backed by a major Amazon investment
- **[Claude](/glossary/claude)**: Anthropic's flagship model, available on AWS Bedrock as a managed API
- **[NVIDIA](/glossary/nvidia)**: Primary GPU supplier for AI training, competing with Amazon's custom Trainium chips
